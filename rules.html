Official Rules & Guidelines — Read Carefully

NORMAL SMP — OFFICIAL RULES (Expanded Edition)

Updated: 12/27/2025

1. PvP Rules

PvP is enabled across the server.

Spawnkilling and repeated harassment near spawn are prohibited.

Do not repeatedly target the same player after they attempt to disengage (1–2 kills maximum).

PvP is not allowed inside the Spawn Green Zone.

2. Cheats, Hacks, or Exploits

No hacked clients or unfair mods.

This includes x-ray, kill aura, fly, speed, auto-crystal, player/entity ESP, or minimap ESP.

Performance/QoL mods (Optifine, Sodium, etc.) are allowed.

Exploiting bugs or glitches is punishable by ban.

Duping of any kind is strictly prohibited.

If you discover a bug, report it — abusing it is punishable.

3. Raiding, Griefing & Storage

Raiding is allowed.

Griefing is allowed outside of spawn areas.

Chests, barrels, shulkers, and item frames may be looted.

Ender chests are personal only — no team sharing.

Staff will not replace lost or stolen items.

4. Spawn Protection

Griefing near spawn is restricted.

No lavacasts, massive TNT damage, or severe terrain destruction around spawn.

Spawn Green Zone is protected — no building or breaking.

Spawn Red Zone allows PvP but still no excessive destruction.

5. Restricted PvP Weapons

The following are BANNED in PvP:

  • End crystals
  • TNT minecarts
  • Beds
  • Respawn anchors

Standard melee and ranged combat only.

6. No Combat Logging

Logging out to avoid death during combat is prohibited.

If confirmed, your inventory may be awarded to your opponent and further punishment may apply.

7. Lag & Performance Abuse

No lag machines or intentional TPS abuse.

Avoid excessive redstone clocks and entity stacking.

AFK farms must be performance-friendly and include off-switches.

Staff may remove lag-heavy builds.

8. Alt Accounts

Limited alt usage is allowed.

Alts may not be used for combat advantage, scouting, or bypassing punishments.

Ban evasion with alts results in permanent bans on all accounts.

9. Map & Tracking Fairness

No external tools that reveal base locations or player coordinates.

Seed-cracking and world download abuse are prohibited.

Coordinate or locator exploits count as cheating.

10. Chat & Community Conduct

Trash talk is allowed — hate speech and real-world threats are NOT.

No racism, slurs, or targeted harassment.

No spam or mass advertising.

No doxxing or sharing personal information.

Do not ping owners/admins — open a ticket instead.

11. Scamming & Impersonation

In-game betrayal and traps are allowed.

Fake staff impersonation is NOT allowed.

Impersonating other players with names/tags is NOT allowed.

12. Trading & Donations

Item trades for VIP purchases are allowed.

Staff middlemen are available to prevent scams.

Scamming in staff-supervised trades is punishable.

13. Trap & Portal Abuse

Do not create unavoidable kill traps at public warps or spawn routes.

Portal traps are allowed but must have survivable counterplay.

Do not permanently block public portals near spawn.

14. Staff Authority & Appeals

Staff intervene only when rules are broken.

Follow staff instructions.

Decisions are final unless overturned on appeal.

Severe or repeated violations may result in permanent bans.

15. Common Sense Clause

Attempting to bypass rules through loopholes counts as rule-breaking.

If it harms fair play or server stability, it is NOT allowed.

16. Playstyle Reminder

Killing, raiding, and griefing are part of normal gameplay outside spawn.

Alliances and betrayal are allowed.

Do not be excessively toxic beyond normal competitive play.
